About Us

"Established in the year 1970, GNGC has carved a niche for itself in the ambit of education. We, at GNGC have commitment to high standards and are focussed on unrivalled academic excellence and concern for individual growth. The state-of-the-art infrastructure, prolific facilities and distinctiveness of the academic programme empowers our students to excel and achieve a fulfilling future. In close collaboration with faculty and staff, our students are nurtured to be discerning and confident young women well equipped to make a meaningful contribution to the real world."
